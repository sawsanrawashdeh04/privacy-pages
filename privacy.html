Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Hayah (Blood Bank Donors) is a mobile application designed to connect blood donors with blood banks and hospitals. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Gender
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Location data
  • Account type (Donor or Blood Bank)
  • Donation-related and request-related information
  • In-app messages and technical usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Verify phone numbers using OTP
  • Enable communication between donors and blood banks
  • Show nearby blood requests based on location
  • Send notifications for urgent blood needs
  • Improve app performance and security

3. Location Usage

Your location may be used to help match donors with nearby requests and improve search and filtering features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud services used for authentication and database functions
  • Authorities when required by law
  • Cases necessary to protect users and system integrity

5. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable measures to protect personal data. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Data may be retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account or data

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age permitted by applicable law without parental or guardian consent.

9. Third-Party Services

The app may use Firebase and related Google services for authentication, notifications, and data storage.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
